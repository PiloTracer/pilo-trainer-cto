# Governance & Risk

**Slug:** `governance-risk`  
**Duration:** 5–7 weeks  
**Level:** CTO accountable for security/compliance/vendors/AI

## Outcomes

- Threat-informed security priorities (not checklist theater)
- Vendor risk posture for critical dependencies
- Practical AI governance for the company’s use cases
- Compliance as product constraint, not surprise

## Modules

### M1 — Security leadership
- Objectives: Own risk register with business language
- Drill: Prioritize top 5 risks for company stage; kill 10 vanity controls
- Sources: NIST CSF (public); OWASP Top 10 (public)

### M2 — Vendor & concentration risk
- Drill: `vendor-risk`
- Sources: Vendor lock-in case studies (public postmortems — cite)

### M3 — AI governance
- Drill: `ai-governance`
- Sources: NIST AI RMF (public); company policy examples (cite)

### M4 — Compliance & audit readiness
- Objectives: Map obligations → engineering controls → evidence
- Exit: Risk register excerpt + vendor drill + AI decision memo

## Assessment

Reject fear-mongering and checkbox compliance without risk ranking.
