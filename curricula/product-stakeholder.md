# Product & Stakeholder

**Slug:** `product-stakeholder`  
**Duration:** 4–6 weeks  
**Level:** CTO partnering with Product / CEO / board

## Outcomes

- Crisp product partnership model (discovery vs delivery)
- Exec updates that create decisions, not theater
- Board narrative: risk, capacity, bets — not vanity metrics

## Modules

### M1 — Product partnership
- Objectives: Shared outcomes, conflict protocols, roadmap ownership
- Drill: Rewrite a messy product/eng conflict into a decision memo
- Sources: Empowered (Cagan); Shape Up (Bach — Basecamp, public)

### M2 — Exec communication
- Objectives: Bottom line up front; options; ask
- Drill: 1-page weekly CTO update for the CEO
- Sources: Public writing on executive communication (cite); Pyramid Principle concepts

### M3 — Board / investor
- Drill: `board-qa`
- Sources: Board meeting best practices from reputable VC/operator writing (cite URLs)
- Exit: Partnership charter + sample board appendix + drill score

## Assessment

Pass if communications force a decision and surface risk honestly.
