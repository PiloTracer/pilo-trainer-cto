# Engineering Leadership

**Slug:** `engineering-leadership`  
**Duration:** 6–8 weeks  
**Level:** CTO / VP Eng with team ≥5

## Outcomes

- Org design that matches strategy (not copy-paste Spotify)
- Hiring scorecards and funnel for one critical role
- Performance system that is fair and decisive
- Culture mechanisms that scale beyond the founder-CTO

## Modules

### M1 — Org design
- Drill: `org-design` — redraw teams for a stated constraint
- Sources: Team Topologies (Skelton/Pais); Accelerate (Forsgren et al.)

### M2 — Hiring
- Drill: `hire-plan` — scorecard + interview loop for one seat
- Sources: Who (Smart); public engineering hiring guides from reputable companies (cite URL)

### M3 — Performance & exits
- Objectives: Calibration, feedback, and when to part ways
- Drill: Case — underperformer after 2 quarters; write the plan
- Sources: Radical Candor (Scott) — principles, not slogans

### M4 — Culture as system
- Objectives: Rituals, on-call norms, quality bars as code/review policy
- Drill: Pick one culture failure mode; design a mechanism (not a poster)
- Exit: Org diagram + hire plan + performance case memo

## Assessment

Pass if drills show constraint-awareness and stakeholder care, not idealism.
